"Every company in the world today needs an OpenClaw strategy."
— Jensen Huang, NVIDIA CEO · GTC, March 16, 2026
OpenClaw is the open-source agentic AI framework — the fastest-growing open-source project in history. CTG's AIMEE stack uses OpenClaw as its Layer 2 agent engine.

AI agents can now do real work — handle customer messages, manage schedules, write reports, triage support tickets. Not chatbots. Not copilots. Actual digital employees with roles, responsibilities, and reporting chains. The CEO of NVIDIA just told every company on earth they need a plan for this.

The problem is simple: most businesses can't build this themselves. The big consultancies want $200K+. The DIY platforms assume you have engineers on staff. There's a massive gap between what's possible and what's accessible.

I've been in managed IT services my whole career — deploying tech for businesses that don't have engineering teams. I've used Power Automate for over 7 years. For the last 5, I've been pulling leads from LinkedIn, RocketReach, Apollo, and Glassdoor with automation bots. For the last 2, every sale I've closed came from a bot. Now I'm pairing that with agentic AI to build something bigger.
